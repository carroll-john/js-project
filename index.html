At Hoopla Salon, we believe great hair should feel like something worth celebrating.

Located on Sydney Road in Brunswick, our salon is built on a simple idea: creating exceptional hair by understanding you, your lifestyle and your hair goals. No ego, no pressure — just a welcoming little world, an organic wine or a fair-trade coffee, and a team that cares.
